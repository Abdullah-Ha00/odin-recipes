Tomato Spaghetti

Dish Description

A rich and vibrant tomato spaghetti dish, featuring a savory sauce made with ripe tomatoes, garlic, and herbs, tossed with al dente pasta for a comforting bite.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes (or 4 fresh tomatoes, diced)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano (or Italian seasoning)
  • ½ teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar (to balance acidity, if needed)
  • ¼ cup pasta water (reserved)
  • ¼ cup fresh basil or parsley (chopped)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve about ½ cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft and translucent. Then, add the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Tomato Sauce: Pour in the crushed tomatoes (or fresh diced tomatoes). Stir in oregano, red chili flakes, salt, black pepper, and sugar (if needed). Simmer for about 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  4. Combine with Pasta: Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss well, adding a little reserved pasta water to help coat the pasta evenly.
  5. Season & Garnish: Remove from heat and mix in fresh basil or parsley. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve: Plate the spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese, extra basil, and a drizzle of olive oil if desired
